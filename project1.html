From CSS to Figma, and back again


  • Overview: UC San Diego embraced a brand update last winter, and it was time the Library updated its digital presence to reflect. The TDX Student Design Team was tasked to redesign the Library homepage to incorporate the institution's new branding elements including color palettes, typography, graphic elements, and photography. Through iterative prototyping and collaboration with our stakeholders, we created several prototypes that showcase our new branding. The redesign is live now, and can be viewed at library.ucsd.edu!

  • Impact: Our efforts resulted in a revamped UC San Diego Library homepage that fully encapsulates the institution's new branding while delivering an enhanced user experience. The homepage serves as a dynamic gateway for students, faculty, and the community to access essential resources and engage with the library's offerings.
